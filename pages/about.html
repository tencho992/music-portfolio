Sierra Montana is a highly acclaimed rhythm Designer, recognized for her outstanding achievements in various fields including rhythm Design, Music Composition, rhythm Branding, rhythmtrack creation, and Audio Installations. With a wealth of leadership experience, she has forged successful collaborations with companies and agencies across the globe, leaving an indelible mark on the world of rhythm and audio innovation.

During her childhood, Sierra Montana embarked on the study of various musical instruments such as the guitar, clarinet, and piano. As an eclectic artist, she harbored a strong desire to defy the limitations imposed by traditional musical notation, venturing into the realm of noise to construct visionary rhythmtracks. Her time as a student at Berklee College allowed her to delve deep into the intricacies of how the human brain responds to rhythms, ultimately unearthing a groundbreaking approach to music composition.

Sierra Montana's insatiable passion for noises and rhythms has driven her to acquire certifications in a diverse range of fields. From delving into the intricacies of the neurobiology of musical cognition to mastering the construction of microphones, she has pursued knowledge across various disciplines.
